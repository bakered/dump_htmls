Red Sea Crisis

Trade between East and West

The charts below plot trade metrics between all ports roughly north-west of the suez canal and all ports roughly south-east of it. More specifically between Europe (minus Russia) and the ports of the Mediterranean on one hand and Asia (Non-Mediterranean), Oceania, and the East coast of Africa including South Africa on the other hand. Each data point is a week: it represents the metrics for the vessels that started their journey in that week.

The Red Sea crisis started following October 7th. The first ship was attacked on November 19th and attacks continued throughout 2024.
